Frequently Asked Questions

What are your operating hours?

Vinayak Pathology Laboratory is open 7 days a week from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM. We also provide 24/7 emergency services for critical tests.

Do you provide home collection services?

Yes, we provide free home collection services within a 5km radius. Our trained phlebotomists will visit your home at your convenient time.

How can I get my test reports?

Test reports are available within 4-24 hours depending on the test. You can collect them from the lab, receive via email, or download from our online portal.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit/debit cards, UPI payments, and online banking. We also accept health insurance for covered tests.

Is your laboratory accredited?

Yes, Vinayak Pathology Laboratory is certified and follows international quality standards for all diagnostic tests.

Do I need to fast before blood tests?

Fasting requirements depend on the specific test. For lipid profile and diabetes tests, 8-12 hours fasting is required. Our staff will inform you about specific requirements when booking.
